The Stuffedbeeps



tell me more...

The phenomenon of Stuffedbeeps started with the D-Sisters: Dido, Dada and Didi. Dido came to earth many years ago and crash landed in lovely Modesto, California. After falling in with the wrong crowd on the mean streets of San Joaquin County, Dido hitched a ride on the back of a trailer-tractor headed to San Jose where she eventually ended up in the toy aisle of a Walgreens and later in my home. Dido's sister Didi came some months afterwards, drawn by her sister's promise of large amounts of partying to be had on the planet Earth. Didi is a party animal. Dada, the older and responsible sister came last, to watch after her wayward siblings and keep them from going too far off into the deep end.

LEFT: The D-Sisters, aren't they adorable? RIGHT: Dido having a soak in the instantpot with the Moocats and some fine mezcal reposado

Some fun facts about the D-Sisters:

  • They speak binary (zeros and ones) in a super fast barely audible series of low and high beeps some have theorized this is why they are called "squeakbeeps"
  • Dido is a techinal genius and finds relaxation reading Cisco switch operation manuals
  • Dada is proficient on both oboe and english horn. She once auditoned for the New York Metropolitan Opera but they refused to hire her because she doesn't speak English
