WHAT THE HELL IS A SQUEAKBEEP?

It is a made up word that refers to furry cute creatures that i know (pets of mine and neighborhood pets), as well as crocheted creations and purely imaginary creatures that have been antrhopomorphized into a cohesive pantheon. Each has their own story and invented personality as well as mythos to it.
